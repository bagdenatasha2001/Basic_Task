How It Works

1.You request a free, no-obligation offer
Answer a few questions online about your home’s features, condition, and upgrades. Be ready with details like your home’s age and square footage.
2. We prepare your offer
To get to your offer price, our local market experts review the sale prices of comparable homes and fine-tune based on your home's unique features and current market trends.
3. Review your offer
We'll make an all-cash offer with no risk of financing fall-through. Your dedicated home advisor will walk through how we calculated your offer, costs, and any questions you have.
4. Assessment
If you love the offer, we’ll complete a free, in-person home assessment to check for any needed repairs. If anything needs to be fixed, you can deduct any repair costs from your net proceeds and let us handle the work.
5. Close and celebrate
Choose your close date so you avoid moving twice or paying two mortgages. You’ll move to your next home stress-free and get paid in days. We’ll take care of getting the home ready to list.
